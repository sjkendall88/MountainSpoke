Our next event!

Our anual "Cycle the Continental Divide" event is just around the corner!

August 18, 2018

We are happy to continue our annual event to "Cycle the Continental Divide," this year is shaping up to be a great year with high attendance. We have a few variations on the route to accomodate our riders at every skill level. We want everyone to come out, enjoy themselves, and stay safe.

The registration fees are: $50 - for adults, $30 - for teenagers, $15 - for youth under 12 years of age, and $150 - for family registration (group limited to 8 members).

As we have mentioned above, we would to keep everyone safe! With your registration package you will receive: a 15% off coupon, that can be used towards your safety gear.
