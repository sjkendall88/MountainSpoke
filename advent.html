Come Join Us on our Adventures!

We offer trail rides twice a day, weekly, and bi-weekly.

  • Daily
    • 5:00 am Sunrise Excursion
    • 6:00 pm Sunset Adventure
  • Weekly
    • 9:00 am Saturday Slalom
  • Bi-Weekly
    • Aug 18 Cycle the Continental Divide
    • Aug 31 Overnight Ride
    • Sep 15 City Run
    • Sep 29 Downhill Course
    • Oct 13 Ridge Traverse
    • Oct 27 Halloween Haunt

Daily

Our daily rides take beautiful trails to reach alternating spots to stop and view the sunrise/sunset. We gather at the shop 2 hours before sunrise/sunset to leave for the trail head. There will be two groups each ride; one for intermediate riders and a second for more advanced riders.

Cost: $5.00 each

Weekly

Our Weekly rides are little more rigorous to elevate the heart rate and experience.

Cost: $20.00 each

Bi-Weekly

Our bi-weekly adventures make a little easier to go out with your family or group and just have a good time. You can meet everyone else that is interested in similar events and encourage others along the way.

Cost:

  • $50 - for adults
  • $30 - for teenagers
  • $15 - for youth under 12 years of age
  • $150 - for family registration (group limited to 8 members)
